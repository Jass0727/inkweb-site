Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum contract term for your subscription plans?

Our Starter and Business Growth plans have a minimum 12-month commitment. The E-commerce Pro plan has a minimum 24-month commitment. This allows us to provide a high-quality website without an upfront build cost, spreading the investment over time.

Do I own the website at the end of the contract?

During the subscription term, InkWeb maintains ownership of the design, code, and content. If you wish to take full ownership of the website (and move it to your own hosting/account) after your minimum term, we offer a transparent buyout option. If you cancel before the minimum term, the website remains our property.

What happens if I need major changes or new features?

Minor content and image updates are included in your monthly plan (see plan details for limits). For major redesigns, new complex features, or significant structural changes, we will provide a separate quote. Our goal is to ensure your website evolves with your business needs.

Is domain name registration and email hosting included?

While we assist with connecting your domain, the domain registration fee itself is typically managed and paid for by the client, ensuring you retain full ownership. We can provide guidance on setting up professional email accounts (e.g., via Google Workspace or Microsoft 365).

How does SEO work with a subscription model?

All our plans include basic on-page SEO setup to ensure your website is search engine friendly from day one. Our Business Growth and E-commerce Pro plans include more comprehensive SEO efforts. For advanced, ongoing SEO campaigns, we can discuss tailored strategies and additional services.
